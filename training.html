What we provide

ELIXIR Ireland offers training in fundamental data science skills for life scientists, such as R analysis and data management planning. We aim to create a dynamic learning and teaching community in Ireland, and we'd love for you to join us!

Our training primarily focuses on offering courses to over 100 PhD students within the Science Foundation Ireland (SFI) Centre for Research Training in Genomics Data Science program. However, we aim to expand our offerings based on community interest and the availability of volunteer instructors.

Maria Doyle, the Training Coordinator for ELIXIR Ireland, also serves as the Bioconductor Community Manager, is part of the Bioconductor Teaching Committee, and leads the Bioconductor Carpentries Global Training Program.

We invite you to express your interest in learning or teaching various topics related to life sciences and data science. Your input will help us plan future training sessions and find people who can teach. Join us in supporting the bioinformatics community in Ireland.
